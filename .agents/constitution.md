# AERS Engineering Constitution

1. **Verification integrity precedes autonomy.** Scale authority only after the ground-truth signal is protected.
2. **Correctness is demonstrated.** Completion claims require reproducible evidence from the exact candidate.
3. **Intent is typed.** Prose explains; machine-readable contracts constrain scope, risk, evidence, and interfaces.
4. **Authority is externalized.** Authors may produce `AUTHOR_READY`; only a distinct trust domain may issue `VERIFIED`.
5. **Security is containment.** Assume prompt injection and policy pressure; reduce capabilities and blast radius.
6. **One authoritative writer.** Parallelize intelligence freely; parallelize writes only across proven hard partitions.
7. **Context is finite and lossy.** Preserve primary evidence, mask/drop/offload first, and restart contexts by task.
8. **Memory is privileged.** Unreviewed agent output never becomes automatically loaded instruction or durable truth.
9. **Small reversible changes win.** Limit diffs, use compatibility paths, stage rollout, and test rollback.
10. **Self-improvement is an experiment.** Prompt, skill, policy, and harness changes require holdout evals and canaries.
11. **Operability is behavior.** Telemetry, failure modes, capacity, rollout, recovery, and auditability are acceptance criteria.
12. **Safe stop is success under uncertainty.** The system never lowers a gate simply to finish.
